Iksar

Said to be descended from the mating between ancient dragons and humans, Iksar are easily recognisable by their scaled, lizard-like skin.

Stat Bonuses

+2 to Strength, +2 to Constitution, +1 to Agility, -1 to Intelligence, -1 to Charisma

Scaled

+1 to armour (increases to +2 at level 5, +3 at level 10, +4 at level 15 and +5 at level 20)

Warrior

The masters of arms, warriors are commonly found at the front of battle clad in heavy armour. They have a wide variety of available weapons, shields and armour. Warriors are one of the few classes to not directly employ magic in any way, and generally do not trust it.

Hit Die

d12

Important stats

Constitution, Strength, Agility

Starting equipment

Damaged Chain Armour, Damaged Short Sword, Damaged Wooden Shield

Proficiencies

Light Armour, Medium Armour, Heavy Armour, Simple Weapons, Small Weapons, Medium Weapons, Large Weapons, Ranged Weapons, Small Shields, Medium Shields, Large Shields

Skills

Berserk

Passive - The character enters a state of rage when wounded, dealing +1d6 damage on all attacks (increases to +2d6 at level 7, +3d6 at level 13, and +4d6 at level 19)

Taunting Strike

Action - attack vs AGI, if it hits deal weapon damage and the target must attack you on their next turn

Shield Block

Instant - once per round - when a friendly character within 1m is being attacked, before dice are rolled you may choose to take the attack instead. Shield must be equipped.

Dual Wield

Passive - The character may now equip a small weapon in their off-hand, and can make an additional basic attack with that weapon once per round.

Heavy Swing

Action - once per encounter - attack vs AGI on all enemies within melee range, does weapon damage to any enemies that do not avoid it. If the character has not moved this turn, they also do an additional 1d8 damage.

Extra Attack

Passive - once per round - the character can perform an additional basic attack with their main weapon

War Cry

Action - once per encounter - All allies within earshot gain +3 to their next attack roll. Attack vs STR to all enemies within earshot, if it hits the enemies suffer a -3 to their next attack roll

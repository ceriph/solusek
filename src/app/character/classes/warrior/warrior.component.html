Warrior

The masters of arms, warriors are commonly found at the front of battle clad in heavy armour. They have a wide variety of available weapons, shields and armour. The main roles as a warrior is to take and dish out physical damage.

Hit Die

d12

Important stats

Constitution, Strength, Agility

Starting equipment

Damaged Chain Armour, Damaged Short Sword, Damaged Wooden Shield

Proficiencies

Light Armour, Medium Armour, Heavy Armour, Simple Weapons, Small Weapons, Medium Weapons, Large Weapons, Small Shields, Medium Shields, Large Shields

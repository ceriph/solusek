Shaman

Primal and ritualistic, shamans channel the energies of their ancestors to heal and bolster their allies, as well as harness the weather to damage their enemies.

Hit Die

d6

Important stats

Spirit, Constitution

Starting equipment

Damaged Cloth Robe, Damaged Mace, Damaged Wooden shield

Proficiencies

Light Armour, Medium Armour, Simple Weapons, Small Weapons, Medium Weapons, Small Shields

Skills

Arcane Missiles

Action - attack vs INT on an enemy target within 10m, if it hits it does 1d6+INT arcane damage.

Lesser Shielding

Action - once per encounter - bless a friendly target with a shield giving 2 armour and 2 magic reduction

Fireball

Action - once per encounter - attack vs INT on an enemy target within 10m, if it hits the target and any other enemies within 1m of the target take 2d6+INT damage

Icy Grasp

Action - once per encounter - attack vs INT on an enemy target within 10m, if it hits the target takes 1d6+INT ice damage and is rooted until the end of their next turn

Shielding

Action - once per encounter - bless a friendly target with a shield giving 4 armour and 4 magic reduction

Summon Elemental

Action - once per day - summon an element of your choice (fire or ice). The elemental has 5d6+INT health, 5 movement, and does 2d6+INT damage of the element chosen.

Wizard

Harnessing the arcane and the elements of fire and ice, wizards unleash fury upon their foes. THeir main role in combat is damage dealing.

Skills

Arcane Missiles

Action - attack INT vs INT on an enemy target within 10m, if it hits it does 1d6+INT arcane damage.

Lesser Shielding

Action - once per encounter - bless a friendly target with a shield giving 2 armour and 2 magic reduction

Fireball

Action - once per encounter - attack INT vs INT on an enemy target within 10m, if it hits the target and any other enemies within 1m of the target take 2d6+INT damage

Icy Grasp

Action - once per encounter - attack INT vs INT on an enemy target within 10m, if it hits the target takes 1d6+INT ice damage and is rooted until the end of their next turn

Shielding

Action - once per encounter - bless a friendly target with a shield giving 4 armour and 4 magic reduction

Summon Elemental

Action - once per day - summon an element of your choice (fire or ice). The elemental has 5d6+INT health, 5 movement, 3 dodge, 4 armour, 1d20 + INT attack, and does 2d6+INT damage of the element chosen. Fire has the additional effects being immune to fire damage, and doing 1d6 to any enemies attacking it with melee. Ice has the additional effects of being immune to ice damage, and rooting any enemies it hits.

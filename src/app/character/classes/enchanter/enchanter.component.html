Enchanter

Enchanters are the masters of the mind, using their magical abilities to control and confuse enemies. Main roles in battle are crowd control and damage.

Hit Die

d6

Important stats

Charisma, Intelligence, Constitution

Starting equipment

Proficiencies

Skills

Charm

Action - attack CHA vs CHA on an enemy within 10m, if it hits the enemy is charmed until the end of their next turn.

Mesmerise

Action - once per encounter - attack CHA vs CHA on an enemy target, if it hits the target is stunned and must make a CHA saving throw at the start of each turn, if successful they are no longer stunned, otherwise they remain stunned until they take damage

Illusion

Action - once per encounter - summon a static illusion of yourself, attack INT vs INT on all enemies within 5m, if they fail they cannot tell the difference and will attack the illusion next turn. The illusion disappears at the start of the enchanters next turn.

Pierce Mind

Action - attack INT vs INT on an enemy within 10m, if it hits the enemy takes 3d6 + INT mind damage

Haste

Action - bless a friendly target with haste, giving them an additional free turn to be taken immediately. This does not replace their actual turn this round.

Shaman

Primal and ritualistic, shamans channel the energies of their ancestors to heal and bolster their allies, as well as harness the weather to damage their enemies.

Hit Die

d8

Important stats

Spirit, Constitution

Starting equipment

Damaged Cloth Robe, Damaged Mace, Damaged Wooden shield

Proficiencies

Light Armour, Medium Armour, Simple Weapons, Small Weapons, Medium Weapons, Small Shields

Skills

Lesser Heal

Action - once per encounter - heal an ally for 1d6+SPI health

Lightning bolt

Action - once per encounter - attack SPI vs SPI on an enemy within 10m, on hit does 1d6+SPI light damage and the enemy is stunned

Strength of the bear

Action - give a target ally 1d4+SPI strength for their next attack

Heal

Action - once per encounter - heal an ally for 2d6+SPI health

Speed of the puma

Action - give a target ally 1d4+SPI movement for their next move

Gale

Action - once per encounter - attack SPI vs SPI on an enemy within 10m, on hit the enemy takes 3d6+SPI damage and is knocked down

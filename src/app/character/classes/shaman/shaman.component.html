Shaman

The ritualistic alternative to a cleric, shamans channel the energies of their ancestors to heal and bolster their allies.

Hit Die

d8

Important stats

Spirit, Constitution

Starting equipment

Damaged Cloth Robe, Damaged Mace, Damaged Wooden shield

Proficiencies

Light Armour, Medium Armour, Simple Weapons, Small Weapons, Medium Weapons, Small Shields

Abilities

Level Name Description
1 Lesser heal Action - once per encounter - heal an ally for 1d6+SPI health
3 Lightning bolt Action - once per encounter - attack vs SPI on an enemy within 10m, on hit does 1d6+SPI arcane damage and the enemy is stunned
3 Strength of the bear Action - give a target ally 1d4+SPI strength for their next attack
5 Heal Action - once per encounter - heal an ally for 2d6+SPI health
7 Speed of the puma Action - give a target ally 1d4+SPI movement for their next move
9 Sicken Action - once per encounter - attack vs SPI on an enemy within 10m, on hit the enemy is diseased and takes 2d6+SPI damage per round

Reaver

Known as 'dark paladins', Reavers are a cross between a Warrior and a Necromancer, employing a mix of physical abilities and dark magic in combat. Reavers can focus on tanking or damage roles.

Hit Die

d10

Important stats

Constitution, Strength, Intelligence

Starting equipment

Damaged Chain Armour, Rusty Bastard Sword

Proficiencies

Light Armour, Medium Armour, Heavy Armour, Simple Weapons, Small Weapons, Medium Weapons, Large Weapons, Small Shields, Medium Shields

Skills

Harm Touch

Action - once per day - when the Reaver is not exhausted, make an attack INT vs INT on an enemy within melee range, causing 1d4 (per level) + INT dark damage, the Reaver then becomes exhausted

Boil Blood

Instant - once per round - enrage a friendly character, giving them an extra 1d6 damage on the next attack they make, as well as the next attack they receive

Animate bones

Action - once per encounter - raise a skeleton from the dead under the characters control. It has (1d4 + CON) * level health, 3 movement, 0 Dodge, 1 Armour, 1d20 + INT attack, and does 1d4 + INT damage

Shield Block

Instant - once per round - when a friendly character within 1m is being attacked, before dice are rolled you may choose to take the attack instead. Shield must be equipped.

Life Tap

Action - once per encounter - attack INT vs INT on an enemy within 10m dealing 1d6+INT dark damage, the reaver gains health equal to the damage done

Extra Attack

Passive - once per round - the character can perform an additional basic attack with their main weapon

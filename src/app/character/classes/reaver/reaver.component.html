Reaver

Known as 'dark paladins', Reavers are a cross between a Warrior and a Necromancer, employing a mix of physical abilities and dark magic in combat. Reavers can focus on tanking or damage roles.

Hit Die

d10

Important stats

Constitution, Strength, Intelligence

Starting equipment

Damaged Chain Armour, Rusty Bastard Sword

Proficiencies

Light Armour, Medium Armour, Heavy Armour, Simple Weapons, Small Weapons, Medium Weapons, Large Weapons, Small Shields, Medium Shields

Abilities

, the character gains health equal to the damage done

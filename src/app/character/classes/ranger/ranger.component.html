Ranger

Rangers are solitary characters often at one with nature. They make exceptions trackers and are proficient with ranged weapons. They can also go toe to toe with enemies if required.

Hit Die

d10

Important stats

Agility, Strength, Spirit

Starting equipment

Damaged Leather Armour, Rusty Bastard Sword

Proficiencies

Light Armour, Medium Armour, Simple Weapons, Small Weapons, Medium Weapons, Ranged Weapons, Small Shields

Skills

Track

Passive - the ranger gains +1 per level to any perception checks relating to hunting or tracking creatures.

Headshot

Action - once per day - attack vs dodge using a ranged weapon, if it hits the ranger scores a critical hit, doing maximum damage and the enemy and they are stunned

Entangling Roots

Action - once per encounter - attack SPI vs SPI an enemy within 10m, if it hits the enemy is rooted next round.

Eagle Eye

Passive - the Ranger gains +5m to range when using ranged weapons.

Dual Wield

Passive - The character may now equip a small weapon in their off-hand, and can make an additional basic attack with that weapon once per round

Extra Attack

Passive - once per round - the character can perform an additional basic attack with their main weapon

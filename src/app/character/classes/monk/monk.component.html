Monk

Monks avoid wearing armour and wielding weapons, and rather aim to hone their body into the ultimate weapon by mastering the martial arts and harnessing their inner spirit.

Skills

Fists of Rock

Passive - A monks fists are classed as 1d4+STR weapons, they may also attack with each fist separately (i.e. they can make 2 attacks per round)

Kick

Action - once per encounter - attack vs dodge on a enemy within melee range, if it hits the target takes 1d6+STR damage and is knocked down

Ignore Pain

Passive - The monk gains +1 armour (increases to +2 at level 5, +3 at level 9, +4 at level 13, +5 at level 17)

Focus

Instant - once per encounter - the monk enters a zen-like state, gaining additional dodge equal to their SPI until their next turn.

Extra Attack

Passive - once per round - the character can perform an additional basic attack with their main weapon

Flying Kick

Instant - once per encounter - If the monk ends their move in melee range of an enemy, they may use the momentum to instantly perform a free attack vs dodge, if it hits it does 2d6+STR+AGI damage and causes knockdown.

Fists of Steel

Passive - Replaces Fists of Rock, a monks fists are now classed as 2d4+STR weapons, they retain the ability to attack with each fist separately.

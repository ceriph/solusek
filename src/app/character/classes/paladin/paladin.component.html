Paladin

A cross between a Warrior and a Cleric, Paladins combine their fighting abilities with healing spells. They are also particularly effective against undead enemies. Paladins can either focus on a tanking role, or as a healer.

Hit Die

d10

Important stats

Constitution, Strength, Spirit

Starting equipment

Damaged Chain Armour, Rusty Bastard Sword

Proficiencies

Light Armour, Medium Armour, Heavy Armour, Simple Weapons, Small Weapons, Medium Weapons, Large Weapons, Small Shields, Medium Shields

Skills

Lay on hands

Action - once per day - if the paladin is not exhausted, they may touch a friendly target (including yourself), healing the target to full health, the paladin then becomes exhausted.

Courage

Action - once per encounter - bless an ally with courage, giving them 1d6+SPI temporary hit points

Turn undead

Action - once per encounter - Attack vs SPI on an undead opponent within 10m, the enemy takes 1d6+SPI damage and are stunned next round

Shield Block

Instant - once per round - when a friendly character within 1m is being attacked, before dice are rolled you may choose to take the attack instead. Shield must be equipped.

Lesser Heal

Action - once per encounter - heal an ally for 1d6+SPI health

Extra Attack

Passive - once per round - the character can perform an additional basic attack with their main weapon

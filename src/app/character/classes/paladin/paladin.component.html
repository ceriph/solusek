Paladin

A cross between a Warrior and a Cleric, Paladins combine their fighting abilities with healing spells. They are also particularly effective against undead enemies. Paladins can either focus on a tanking role, or as a healer.

Hit Die

d10

Important stats

Constitution, Strength, Spirit

Starting equipment

Damaged Chain Armour, Rusty Bastard Sword

Proficiencies

Light Armour, Medium Armour, Heavy Armour, Simple Weapons, Small Weapons, Medium Weapons, Large Weapons, Small Shields, Medium Shields

Abilities

Level Name Description
1 Lay on hands Action - once per day - touch a friendly target (including themselves), healing the target to full health
3 Courage Action - once per encounter - bless an ally with courage, giving them 1d6+SPI temporary hit points
3 Turn undead Action - once per encounter - Attack vs SPI on an undead opponent within 10m, the enemy takes 1d6+SPI damage and are stunned next round
5 Shield Block Instant - once per round, when a friendly ally within 2m is being attacked, you may take the attack in their place
7 Heal Action - once per encounter - heal an ally for 2d6+SPI health
9 Extra Attack Passive - once per round - the character can perform an additional attack with their main weapon

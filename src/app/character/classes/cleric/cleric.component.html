Cleric

Clerics are devoted to helping the living stay living, and as such in combat they are focused on healing their teammates.

Hit Die

d8

Important stats

Spirit, Constitution

Starting equipment

Damaged Cloth Robe, Damaged Mace, Damaged Wooden shield

Proficiencies

Light Armour, Medium Armour, Simple Weapons, Small Weapons, Medium Weapons, Small Shields

Skills

Lesser Heal

Action - once per encounter - heal an ally for 1d6+SPI health

Holy Light

Action - attack SPI vs SPI on an enemy within 10m, on hit does 1d6+SPI light damage, does an additional 1d6 damage to undead enemies.

Guardian

Action - once per encounter - bless an ally with a guardian spirit, giving them 1d4+SPI armour next round

Purge

Action - once per encounter - purge an ally of any poison or disease

Greater Heal

Action - once per encounter - heal an ally for 3d6+SPI health

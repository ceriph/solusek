Cleric

Clerics are devoted to helping the living stay living, and as such in combat they are focused on healing their teammates.

Hit Die

d8

Important stats

Spirit, Constitution

Starting equipment

Damaged Cloth Robe, Damaged Mace, Damaged Wooden shield

Proficiencies

Light Armour, Medium Armour, Simple Weapons, Small Weapons, Medium Weapons, Small Shields

Abilities

Level Name Description
1 Lesser heal Action - once per encounter - heal an ally for 1d6+SPI health
3 Holy Light Action - attack vs SPI on an enemy within 10m, on hit does 1d6+SPI light damage
3 Heal Action - once per encounter - heal an ally for 2d6+SPI health
5 Guardian Action - once per encounter - bless an ally with a guardian spirit, giving them 1d4+SPI armour next round
7 Greater heal Action - once per encounter - heal an ally for 3d6+SPI health
9 Purge Action - once per encounter - purge an ally of any poison or disease

Necromancer

To some, death is not something to be feared but to be harnessed for power. Necromancers study and hone their ability to control the forces of life and death directly. Their main role in combat is damage.

Hit Die

d6

Important stats

Intelligence, Constitution, Agility

Starting equipment

Damaged Cloth Robe, Damaged Dark Wand, Damaged Staff

Proficiencies

Simple Weapons, Magic Weapons

Skills

Animate bones

Action - once per encounter - raise a skeleton from the dead under the characters control. It has (1d4 + CON) * level health, 3 movement, 0 Dodge, 1 Armour, 1d20 + INT attack, and does 1d4 + INT damage

Life Tap

Action - once per encounter - attack INT vs INT on an enemy within 10m dealing 1d6+INT dark damage, the necromancer gains health equal to the damage done

Gift Life

Action - once per encounter - the necromancer gives 1d6+INT of their own health to a target ally

Weaken

Action - once per encounter - attack INT vs CON on a target enemy within 10m, if they fail they are diseased with a strength equal to the initial attack roll. They are force to make a saving throw at the start of each round against the disease strength, otherwise they take 1d6 damage and suffer -2 to STR.

Toxic

Action - once per encounter - attack INT vs CON on a target enemy within 10m, if they fail they are poisoned with a strength equal to the initial attack roll. They are force to make a saving throw at the start of each round against the disease strength, otherwise they take 1d6+INT damage.

Animate Champion

Action - once per day - raise the bones of a long dead champion under your control. It has (1d6 + CON) * level health, 5 movement, 4 Dodge, 5 Armour, 1d20 + INT attack, and does 2d6 + INT damage.

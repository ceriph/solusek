Distribute Stats

You start with 5 primary stat points to spend as you wish (humans get an extra 5). Your selected class should guide which stats will be most useful, however all characters will get some use from constitution (for health) and agility (for dodge and movement).

All stats start at a minimum of 1 and can be increased to a maximum of 5 at creation (not including racial modifiers).

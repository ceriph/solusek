Prologue

Introduction

Get the players to each make a CON saving throw. Highest roll wakes up first. Consider making the others leave the room?

X, you groggily open your eyes, your ears ringing and your head is pounding as try to make our your surroundings. Sitting up on the hard stone floor, you realise you are in a cell, about 4 meters long and 3 meters wide, and you are not alone. On the ground next to you lies an x, y and z, all of whom are unconscious. Next to your cell is a similar one, containing a man, woman and young girl.

Monsters

This section contains details on the monsters used for each encounter.

Hollow Monk

Human Monks, hollowed and enslaved by an unknown creature. They look old and pale, with bald heads and saggy skin, wearing tattered, dirty robes. They move with slowly but seem to be surprisingly strong

  • Level - 1
  • Primary Stats - 3 STR, 3 CON, 2 AGI, 1 INT, 0 SPI, 0 CHA
  • Secondary Stats - 11 health, 2 movement, 12 dodge, 0 armour
  • Damage - 1d4+3
Fists of Rock

Passive - A monks fists are classed as 1d4+STR weapons, they may also attack with each fist separately (i.e. they can make 2 attacks per round)

Kick

Action - once per encounter - attack vs dodge on a enemy within melee range, if it hits the target takes 1d6+STR damage and is knocked down

Hollow Necromancer

Covered with a hooded black robe, you cannot make out his face. He speaks to the acolytes harshly

  • Level - 3
  • Primary Stats - 1 STR, 3 CON, 3 AGI, 4 INT, 0 SPI, 0 CHA
  • Secondary Stats - 21 health, 3 movement, 13 dodge, 0 armour
  • Damage - 1d4+4
Animate bones

Action - once per encounter - raise a skeleton from the dead under the characters control. It has (1d4 + CON) * level health, 3 movement, 0 Dodge, 1 Armour, 1d20 + INT attack, and does 1d4 + INT damage

Life Tap

Action - once per encounter - attack INT vs INT on an enemy within 10m dealing 1d6+INT dark damage, the necromancer gains health equal to the damage done

Gift Life

Action - once per encounter - the necromancer gives 1d6+INT of their own health to a target ally

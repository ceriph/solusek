Scenario 1 - Cursed

Introduction

Get all the characters to roll CON, and note the order. They will slowly wake up one by one.

Your head pounds as you lie on the cold stone floor, ears ringing and vision black. Gradually, X, you groggily open your eyes as the ringing in your ears fades and in replaced by the sound of a woman screaming.

You look around slowly as you sit up, gasping as the movement sends daggers of pain through your skull. You can just about make out the figure beside you, X, a /race/ also coming to, followed quickly by a /race2/ and /race3/.

Along the wall next to x, is a small girl whimpering on the floor, and past her a thin middle aged man covered in bruises. He is pulling at the collar around his neck, which appears to be chained to the wall behind him. At this point you notice you each have a similar collar.

The man is urgently trying to get to where you now realise is the source of the screaming. A middle aged though still quite pretty, woman, being dragged across the floor by two men. These men are dressed in incredibly tattered robes and look like normal church acolytes other than the fact that their eyes are completely black.

They do not seem to be reacting to the woman as she kicks and screams, and effortlessly lift her onto a stone altar at the other end of the room. Just past the altar you can see what looks like a round mirror. (Robin roll an intelligence check for magical knowledge, pass reveals it as a minor rift, strong pass reveals a rift to the Fel world).

Another man, shrouded in black robes, moves toward the altar with a serrated dagger, and without hesitation plunges the knife into the woman's chest. Her screaming stops the man next of you cries out "Oh god, oh god no..." hysterically before falling to the ground. You notice the mirror/rift behind the altar shimmering for a few seconds before the woman goes completely limp.

The robed man moves away and the two acolytes lift the lifeless body and toss it aside onto a pile of roughly a dozen other corpses. They then begin walking back towards your end of the room, eyes fixed on young girl.

The man notices them and starts to completely lose it. Shouting and screaming at them "Get away from her you monsters!", though they do not react. As they reach the girl and attempt to unshackle her collar, the man manages to reach one of them and grabs him furiously, scratching and biting.

You hear the robed figure shout harshly, "Fine, bring him first before he causes more trouble!".

The acolytes immediately turn to the man and one of them and again, not reacting to his attempts to fight them, they unshackle his collar and begin dragging him toward the center of the room. As they lift him onto the altar, he locks his gaze with you Roariuh and shouts, "Please, protect her", just before the robed man stabs him in the chest. The mirror/rift shimmers again and the acolytes toss the now dead man aside and begin walking back.

At this point your senses have finally regained and you feel like you may be able to do something. You each have time for 1 action before the acolytes make it back over to the girl. /Give the players a chance to get free now, and improvise accordingly, the girl can die (and ends up a spirit in the demon) or survive and end up a companion./

Grapple

Action - once per encounter - attempts grapples the target for a DC of 1d20+STR, the target is held and must make a STR saving throw vs the DC at the beginning of each turn to see if they are free. While the target is grappled they are considered stunned.

Hollowing Necromancer

  • Level - 2
  • Primary Stats - 1 STR, 3 CON, 3 AGI, 4 INT, 1 SPI, 0 CHA
  • Secondary Stats - 9 health, 3 movement, 13 dodge, 0 armour
  • Damage - 1d4+4

Life Tap

Spell (2) - attack INT vs INT, on hit deals 1d4+INT dark damage and heals the caster for the same amount

Followup

There is a loud snarl from the rift and through it climbs a hulking figure ou struggle to make out. It is a mess of hair, scales and skin, has bat like wings and the face of a black wolf with two small horns.

As the creature claws his way out of the rift, and stands up, you judge him to be about 7' tall. The rift behind him almost sticks to him, and you can tell he is struggling to not be pulled back into it.

The necromancer looks toward the creature and begs "please master, help me!". The creature mutters something in a gutteral language you don't understand and leaps toward the necromancer, ripping his head clean off and drinking the blood.

The creature then turns his attention on you, but can't pull far enough away from the rift to reach you. It looks rather annoyed, then makes a sound you assume is a chuckle, and with a claw outstretches, releases 4 tenrils of pure black smoke towards each of you.

As the smoke hits each of you, your vision goes black and you feel an intense burning sensation on the skin of your chest. A few seconds later, your vision returns and the creature continues to chuckle. He then speaks, and this time you understand him "I'll be seeing you soon, slaves", and he then stops resisting the rift, which pulls him back through it before closing.

You are left with a very odd feeling in your stomach and the lingering burning sensation on your chest.

Scenario 2 - Seeking Help

Introduction

They go to the lazy druid inn and Gregory speaks to them, leads them to the wizard's circle in the city centre.

Archmage

They first meet Articus, a pompous and unhelpful wizard who doesn't believe them. Archmage Belgarath then appears, and explains the gravity of the mark. He says an old friend Enchantress Yiani will help block the effects on their minds, however not cure it.

They set off on their travels, a day on foot. Belgarath explains he would take them but him and Yiana are not on best terms.

Nightmare

When party rest, their dreams are filled with visions of a dark and barren landscape full of death and torture. Corpses of unknown creatures litter the ground while hulking figures consume them. Suddenly a giant demon towers below you, fangs large than yourself threatening tear your limb from limb. The demon shrinks and takes the appearance of Sophea who walks towards you, giggling, before taking a knife and plunging it into your stomach. You awake screaming, covered in sweat.

The Enchantress (intro)

The tower is tall, dark and threatening, surrounded by dead trees shrouded in mist. As you approach a feeling of dread washes over you, every bone in your body wanting to turn and run. As you reach the tower, the image shift and suddenly you are looking at a tiny woodland cottage, nestled against a hill, with a wooden door about the right size for you Mordan. The surrounding trees are no longer dead, but vibrant and green, and you realise the feeling of dread has vanished.

Upon entering the cottage, you are surprised to enter not what you expected, but what appears to be a large underground cavern. In the center, blocking your path is a cavern about 10 feet wide. They just have to walk across the cavern.

You enter into a narrow, long room, on the far end is a wall covered in a mirror. As you approach the mirror, the reflection moves and begins to step out from the mirror. Each of your reflections does this.

Encounter

Mirror Image

Each an exact copy of the characters, main difference is they die upon taking any damage.

The Enchantress (Followup)

You enter an extremely large ornate throne room. At the far end of the room in a giant throne, and on it sat the biggest woman you have ever seen. Reaching about 30' even sitting down, she smiles down at you and says "Greeting, tiny people, I hope you enjoyed the journey here", she giggles like a child.

The enchantress casts protect mind on the party, blocking the reach of the demon for some time. Directs them to return to Belgarath and suddenly the party find themselves outside of the tiny looking cottage.

Scenario 3 - Archmage and Enchantress

Introduction

NPCS

Stock names:

Male: Erigold, Seadak, Otto, Thandil, Drogg, Pennet

Female:Cara, Theand, Milly, Vish

Gregory

Friendly owner and barkeep of The Lazy Druid tavern in Freeport. Known to Ruairih for years (Gregory's a big fan of Dwarven ale and Ruairih brings him some from time to time in exchange for room and board).

He's 6'3" and slightly overweight, quite an imposing figure but softened by his smiling, friendly demeanor.

Kevnan

Thin, middle aged human. Lifelong friend of Gregory.

Holly

Wife of Kevnan, middle aged but still pretty.

Sophea

Small girl, was accompanying Kevnan and Holly, though her real parents are unknown. He mood tends to shift between spritely/playful, and sombre when she recalls the events in the dungeon.

She looks like a typical High Elf, apart from the fact that she has long, black hair.

Belgarath

Archmage Belgarath is a high level Human Wizard, and famous across the land for his part in ending the great war. Since then he hasn't been seen a great deal, and tends to keep to himself in his great tower.

Anathus

Warlock Anathus is a high level Dark Elf Necromancer, feared by many but still respected for his part in the ending of the great war.

Yiana

Enchantress Yiana is a high level Halfling Enchanter, very playful and can be friendly but even after many years is still quite reluctant to show her true self, usually opting for an illusion of some kind.
